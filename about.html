My Background

I was born and raised in Cincinnati, Ohio, but I currently live in Seattle, Washington. I moved to Seattle for the Ada Developers Academey program to make it easier when internships begin and for after it ends. So far I'm loving this prgram and I really like the challenging assignments. It honestly reminds me of my science teacher in elementary school who was the first one to give me challenging problems and help me find my love for math and science. Thanks to her, her influence is what made me want to be an engineer and helped me grow by making me compete in science competitions. Every year she made do a completely different design or topic than what I did in previous years to show me that there are unlimited ways to solve problems. At first she only had me sign up for egg drop competitions. Then once I got first place in that the next year she added toothpick bridges, rubberband airplanes, mouse trap cars, then lastly the science fair. Each year she added one of those competitions onto what I did the year before, but she constantly made sure that it was something I wanted and that I wasn't overwhelmed. The instructors in this program remind of me of her with their kindness and drive to make us stronger people and developers.
