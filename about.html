ABOUT ME

I feel fortunate to have amazing peers and instructors who selflessly and tirelessly strive to help, encourage and support every student. Truly grateful for this experience. I am excited to show you what I have learned in the past few months!

I have quickly learnt new technical skills, demonstrated good team building skills, solid time management, and excelled within a fast-paced start-up environment.


I am always looking for a challenge and an opportunity to creatively solve complex real-world problems.
