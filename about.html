Hi! I never really know what to say about me. Coming up with things that don't sound like I just learned to write last week is hard. I guess, I'll start at the VERY begining. I was born in Lima - Peru, way back in the late 80's, when big hair was a thing and the internet was not. During my early years computers were a thing that I saw once or twice a week while in school. So not too new, but not exactly familiar. Life went on and in 2004 my family migrated to the US. Once here I we settled in North Idaho were I had to promptly learned to speak English as the end of highschool was coming ever closer. I graduated highschool and went on to college from which I graduated in 2011 with a degree in psychology. My husband and I moved to the Seattle area the year prior.
