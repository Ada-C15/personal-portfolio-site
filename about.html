ABOUT ME

HALISA SAIPULLA

Full Stack Development Student at Ada Developer Academy


Hello! My name is Halisai Saipulamu, my pronouns are she/her, and I’m excited to begin a software developer internship through Ada Developers Academy. Before delving into coding I studied chemical engineering at the Shanghai Jiao Tong University and energy and nuclear engineering at the Polytechnic University of Turin .


My chemistry background developed my curiosity to unknown challenges, which improved my critical thinking and creativity. I also have retail experiences which gave me the opportunity to collaborate with excellent team members and enhance customer facing effective communication skills. When I moved to the United States four years ago, I was exposed to coding for the very first time by my coworker. The sense of accomplishment, the thrill of managing my technical expertise to create a product from scratch and the excitement of seeing my potential appealed to me, which convinced me to start my programming journey.


I’m proud to be the first Uyghur to be part of the Ada community, and got the chance to pursue my dream. I look forward to applying my tech proficiency to make the tech accessible to more underrepresented communities and empower them to accomplish more.
