MY TREK TO SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT

I am a visual artist with an extensive 3-D design, ceramics, and visual arts background. My background includes designing and delivering 3-D curricula to a range of students from the university level at the University of Washington to middle schools in NYC. As a ceramics material specialist, I worked with art professionals in NYC honing my ability to collaboratively scaffold complex social and artistic concepts for both students and the public. I am passionate about distilling complex projects into manageable components. This vision for design conception and execution drives my desire to contribute to socially responsible projects. In 2021, I joined Ada Developers Academy where I am driven to learn fullstack development to initiate, inform and deploy positive environmental and social products. I am elated to join the global movement advancing creative tech solutions to complex social and environmental dilemmas. I see a future where technology is used to promote health for our planet and all of its inhabitants.
