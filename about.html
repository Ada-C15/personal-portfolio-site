About Me

I decided to pursue my Bachelor’s in Instructional Education, and prior to Ada, I worked at a B2B SaaS company as a Product Operations Manager to expand into EMEA, and APAC countries.

While working as a liaison between the developers, business partners, and end users, I realized that I enjoyed technical topics more, so I decided to revive my childhood dream and pick up coding once again. Now here I am, a proud student of Ada Developers Academy.

In addition to what I’m learning at Ada, my prior job experience with security and product API’s, which heavily utilized research, experimentation and data analytics have positively impacted my learnings, and I hope to expand on my knowledge during internship, as I get the opportunity to explore both the back and front end to more deeply discover where my skills and interests align.
