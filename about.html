My name is Ruthie Newman, my pronouns are she/her. I have always had a passion for writing and visual arts. I studied literature, fine arts and social/behavioral studies in university and before endeavoring toward a career in software development, I worked in both social work and medical fields.

I decided to make the transition into software development because I saw the incredible creative potential of software engineering and could not resist following my curiosity. As I have progressed on this path, I have discovered a deep passion for code and I am now inspired to master the art with the hope of contributing code that is of benefit to underrepresented and underserved peoples in tech and beyond.
