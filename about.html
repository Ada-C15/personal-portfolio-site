I come from a visual arts background but have been in the orbit of computers and technology since my childhood. I discovered my passion for coding while doing work that combined design, user experience and a bit of HTML, CSS and Javascript. Prior to Ada, I was following my interest in primarily front-end design by self teaching javaScript, React and Wordpress theme development. I’m looking forward to our months at Ada where I’ll be able to get more in-depth with React state management and context, as well as learn about open source development.

I’m excited by the technological and social possibilities of open source projects, front-end design that is built around accessibility (including linguistic/cultural diversity, accounting for the ‘digital class divide’, and more) and generally learning more about things that interest me. I’m fascinated by making web interfaces inviting for people who use non-latin alphabets, and the technical fixes that are required to do so.
