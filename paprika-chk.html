Paprika Chicken

If done right, this paprika chicken recipe will give you a fast, sweet-spicy, and juicy chicken. Recommendation: Feel free to adjust your spices to fit your tastes!

HOW TO:
  1. Slice Chicken into thin strips. The chicken should be cut into even pieces. (Note: The thinner the chicken the quicker it will cook.)
  2. Prepare an oven tray or deep glass plexiglass to bake the chicken in.
  3. In a bowl mix together Paprika, Salt, Oregano, Pepper, Thyme, Garlic Powder, and Brown Sugar.
  4. In a separate bowl coat the the chicken strips evenly with oil. Then using a spoon or your fingers, sprinkle the mixes spices all over the chicken making sure all sides all evenly covered.
  5. Place the seasoned chicken on the prepared tray or plexiglass.
  6. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes. Check to make sure the chicken is cooked through by cutting and checking the thickest part of the piece.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
