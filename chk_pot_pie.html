Cooking Chicken Pot Pie

Spending more time at home means more time learning to cook! Over time I've come to prefect a couple of recipes. One of these is my favorite chicken pot pie.

HOW TO:
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Chop chicken breasts into strips or small chunks. Heat oil in sauce pan over medium/high heat. Cook chicken for 4 minutes in high heat. season chicken with a pinch of salt and pepper. Cover with 1 cup of water and cook until water has evaporated. Set chicken aside.
  3. Using the same pan add margarine and cook onion until browned over medium heat. Reduce heat and add pepper, flour, and chicken bouillon. Mix well. Add water, milk, vegetables, and crème fraiche.
  4. Pull chicken into small pieces. Add chicken to pan. Cook mixture until it’s mixed well and mixture is thick.
  5. Fill pie dish with your mixture evenly. Use one of the rolled pie crusts to cover the pie. Cut excess from sides and place several holes through dough.
  6. Bake pie in preheated oven for 35 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
