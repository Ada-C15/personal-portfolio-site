Jalapeno Tuna Steaks

Tuna Steaks have become a weekly special at my apartment. This recipe is very easy, quick, and delicious. If you're like me and like a little kick in flavor then I recommend trying this recipe!

HOW TO:
  1. Combine garlic, lemon juice, soy sauce, scallion, sliced jalapenos and jalapeno juice in a container large enough to fit both tuna steaks.
  2. Marinate tuna steaks in mixture for 1 hour. Turn tunas over after 30 minutes.
  3. Grill tunas on oiled cast iron pan for 5 minutes on each side.
  4. Add marinated juice on top of tuna. Cook tuna on first side for an additional minute.
  5. Serve hot with jalapeno slices right on top!
